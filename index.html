About Cloud & Datacenter

Cloud computing allows on-demand access to resources like servers, storage, and networking over the internet. Datacenters are physical facilities housing servers and networking infrastructure to provide services globally. AWS is one of the leading cloud providers powering millions of websites and applications worldwide.
